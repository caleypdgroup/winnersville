Fitness

Crossfit

CrossFit is defined as “constantly varied, functional movements operated at a high intensity.” CrossFit is an all-inclusive fitness program. That means, no stone will be left un-turned when it comes to your fitness.

Crossfit Kids

The Strength & Conditioning programming for 2nd - 5th grade students is designed just for them. Our goal is to get kids generally prepared physically for all of the challenges that life may throw at them. We will increase their athletic ability by covering all aspects of fitness....

YoBilityFit

YoBilityFit combines the best of Yoga, Mobility and high intensity training all in a one hour class. YoBilityFt programming is designed with 20-30 minutes of Yoga and/or Mobility exercises and 20-30 minutes of high intensity workouts everyday. In the YoBilityFit classes we will NOT be doing any weightlifting with a barbell...

What Client’s Say

Hannah Russell / South Georgia Pecan Company

I have been doing Crossfit for about 8 years now and have moved around a lot in that time. I have been a member at over ten different Crossfit gyms. Mostly, the environments all have the same things. Same burpees, same squats, same suck. Crossfit Winnersville has those things (definitely the burpee part), but it also has something much deeper. A mission to build a healthier community. With monthly Faith & Fitness wods, a Beat-the-Streets Program, Yobility classes, nutritional seminars, and countless community fundraisers, Crossfit Winnersville has turned the sport of Crossfit into a ministry. While many other programs are centered around physical and fitness improvements, Crossfit Winnersville offers the opportunity for holistic growth.

Becky Kluball / Community Member

The community aspect of the gym is what keeps me coming back every week.
